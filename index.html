A Daily Ritual, Centuries in the Making

Nilora brings you the softness of nature in its purest form — wild, unrefined Nilotica Shea butter, harvested by women in Northern Uganda and cold-pressed to preserve its rare nutrients. Melt it into your hands. Share it with your child. Feel the difference of butter that heals, hydrates, and soothes — from face to belly to baby skin.

 Rooted in Uganda, Loved by Families

 Our butter is wild-harvested by hand under the Nile sun — no additives, no shortcuts. Every jar supports women-led communities and protects the ancient Nilotica trees of East Africa. This is skincare with soul, crafted by nature and elevated by heritage.
